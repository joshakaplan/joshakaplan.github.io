River Greaser

A documentary style film, following the story of Ric Gundman, a homeless man who lives his life along the LA River. Considering himself a modern samurai, his life is given purpose when he discovers that his lost wife (who was taken by a flash flood) might still be alive.

Starring

Josh Kaplan, Ele Woods, Zack Pearlman, Mark Schroeder, Trian Long-Smith

Directed by

Nick Rasmussen

Written by

Josh Kaplan and Nick Raz
