Josh Kaplan

Director, Writer, Actor

Josh Kaplan is a Los Angeles-based filmmaker and musician from Chicago, Illinois. He wrote, produced, and starred in his first feature film, 'River Greaser', which was independently released in the fall of 2014. He wrote, directed and scored two short films in 2015: 'Momma Rig' and 'Closure'.
